人  
總有些  
過不去的  
坎!

常常  
出現，  
想要  
對話!

那就  
試著  
寫出來吧!

也許  
可以  
解決一些!

---

There are  
always obstacles  
that people  
can't get over!

Appears  
often and  
wants to  
talk!

Then  
try to  
write it!

Maybe  
some can be  
solved!
