詩是一種彈性語言!

寫詩就是  
寫自己的經驗!  
寫自己的生命!  
寫自己的夢想!  
寫自己的精彩!

把自己寫出來!

---

Poetry is a flexible language!

Writing poetry is  
Write our own experience!  
Write our own life!  
Write our own dream!  
Write our own wonderful story!

Write ourself out!
