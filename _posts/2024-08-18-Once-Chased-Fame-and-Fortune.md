在  
生命  
的  
過程  
中，  
追逐  
名利，  
確實  
必要。

畢竟  
這是  
前進  
的  
動力  
之一。

我們  
不用  
刻意  
放棄，  
這樣  
反而  
不人性。

不過  
回頭  
看看，  
自己  
生命  
的  
過程，  
卻是得  
適時  
懂得  
放下。

曾經  
追求，  
但不  
糾結!

---

In the  
process of  
life,  
it is  
indeed necessary  
to pursue  
fame  
and  
fortune.

After all,  
this is  
one of the  
driving forces  
for  
progress.

We don't  
have to  
give up  
deliberately,  
it  
would be  
inhumane.

But looking back,  
we realize that  
in the course of  
our life,  
we  
have to  
know  
how to  
let go  
at the  
right time.

We have  
pursued it,  
but we don't  
struggle with it!
