2024年7月左右。

無意間逛網頁時，發現碩士班時期同實驗室的女同學，在美國擔任博士後研究員。

由於身處國外，也體會到台灣問題的難度，在美國也開始串聯當地台灣人，來為台灣社會發聲。

祝福她即使身處他鄉，也過得好!

祝福她!

---

Around July 2024.

When I accidentally browsed the Internet, I discovered that a female classmate in the same laboratory during my master's class was working as a postdoctoral researcher in the United States.

Since she is living abroad, she also understands the difficulty of the Taiwan issue. She has begun to connect with local Taiwanese in the United States to speak out for Taiwanese society.

I wish her a good life even if she is in a foreign country!

Bless her!
