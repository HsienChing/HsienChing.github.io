下著大雨的夜晚

在快速道路上開車

遇到水窪

加速跟減速似乎都不是好選擇

那就慣性滑行吧!

一路滑過危險地帶

祝福 慣性滑行 行車平安!

祝福!

---

Heavy rainy night

Driving on expressway

Encountered a puddle

Speeding up and slowing down didn't seem to be good choices.

Then let's coast!

Coasting through dangerous areas

Bless us coasting and safe driving!

Bless!
